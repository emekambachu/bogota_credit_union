Commercial Mortgage

Take Advantage of Competitive Real Estate Loan Rates and Quick Closings.

 

We Understand the Value of a Business Opportunity

When it comes to commercial lending opportunities, Bogota Credit Union has led the industry with a steadfast commitment to provide meaningful and flexible solutions – solutions that allow our business customers to take advantage of life’s opportunities. At Bogota Credit Union, we understand the value of opportunity.

Commercial Lending Decisions Made at a Local Level

Competitive rates are just the beginning. As a community bank with a commitment to supporting community businesses, our local decision makers live and work in the markets we serve and you always have access to senior management.

Whether a commercial office building, multi-family apartment, industrial warehouse, retail space or owner-occupied, our lending team works tirelessly to get you to the closing table – making your opportunity our priority. Bogota Credit Union is proud to lend to businesses throughout New Jersey – from Bergen and Hudson Counties to Monmouth and Ocean Counties.

Commercial Mortgages with Key Advantages

Our experienced commercial lending team provides solutions that are customized to your specific needs and the knowledge to guide you through a process that can be challenging. Our commercial mortgage loan amounts range from hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars and feature significant advantages, including:

  • Competitive Rates
  • Long-term Adjustable Rates
  • All Loans Held in Portfolio
  • 25 Year Maximum Amortization & Maturity Dates
  • Up to 70% Loan to Value
  • Easy Application Process
  • Quick Decisions
