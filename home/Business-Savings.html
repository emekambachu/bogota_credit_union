Business Savings Accounts with Benefits

Saving is an important financial component of any successful business. Whether saving for unexpected expenses or growth and expansion plans, Bogota Credit Union can provide a savings account option that is right for you, your business and your savings objectives.

Your Money Earning Interest

Build your business. Build your capital. Earn interest while you save. Your money is always FDIC insured (to applicable limit) and earning interest at competitive rates. In fact, at Bogota Credit Union, we pay our business accounts the same rate of interest as our personal accounts.

e-Statement Savings Accounts

Bogota Credit Union offers businesses the opportunity to open select deposit accounts online. Online accounts typically will carry a higher interest rate.
