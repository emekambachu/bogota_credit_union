Allpoint ATM

Bogota Credit Union expands Surcharge-Free ATM availability for customers through Allpoint Network.

Bogota Credit Union has joined Allpoint Network, which means Bogota Credit Union customers now have access to America’s largest surcharge-free ATM network, with some 55,000 surcharge-free ATMs worldwide. Allpoint Network is an affiliate of Cardtronics, Inc. (NASDAQ: CATM), the world’s largest retail ATM owner.

In addition to Bogota Credit Union’s branch locations and ATMs, with Allpoint Network, Bogota Credit Union customers will never be far away from surcharge-free ATM access to their cash, especially in the United States where Allpoint offers 43,000 cash machines in places they’re already visiting as part of their daily routine. Conveniently located in destination retail outlets including major discount retailers, convenience, grocery and pharmacy stores, Allpoint Network ATMs save customers of Bogota Credit Union time as well as ATM fees.

“By becoming an Allpoint Network member, in an instant Bogota Credit Union grows its surcharge-free ATM footprint to more than double that of even America’s largest bank,” said President and CEO, Joseph Coccaro “People want a bank that delivers the most convenient account access and management options, and that includes cash access. With Allpoint Network surcharge-free ATMs, we can meet our customers cash access needs in a way that works best for them — in the conveniently located stores that are already part of their lives.”

How to find Allpoint Network

Bogota Credit Union customers looking for the nearest surcharge-free Allpoint ATM—including 12,000 locations in Canada, Mexico, Puerto Rico, the United Kingdom, Australia—can do so with their smartphone using the free Allpoint Network ATM locator app or by visiting www.allpointnetwork.com. Once you have found the location, look for the green Allpoint logo on the ATM.
