Mobile Banking

With Bogota Credit Union mobile banking, you can safely and securely deposit a check, monitor account balances, transfer funds, pay bills, set alerts and more right from your mobile device – anytime, almost anywhere. It’s instant account access right at your fingertips and convenient for today’s busy lifestyle.

 

Current mobile banking features include:

  • View Bank Account History, Balance and Activity
  • Transfer Money Between Bogota Credit Union Accounts
  • Mobile Check Deposit
  • Set Email and Text Alerts
  • Bill Pay
  • Pop Money

Mobile Bill Pay

Pay your bills even when you're on the go. Once you enroll in Online Banking, our bill payment setup is quick and easy. Payments are then made anytime right with our mobile app.

Mobile Check Deposit

Mobile Banking makes it easy and convenient to deposit checks anytime, almost anywhere.

PopMoney | Person-to-Person Payment

Enjoy the ability to transfer money between accounts – even if one of the accounts is not a Bogota Credit Union account. Pay back a friend, transfer money to your child's account, and more!

 

Safe and Secure Mobile Banking

Bogota Credit Union is committed to protecting your personal information. Mobile banking is safe, secure and convenient and with our newest version, there is no need to register for mobile banking. As long as you are registered for online banking, simply download our mobile app and start banking on the go today!
