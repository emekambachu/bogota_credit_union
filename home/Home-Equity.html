Meeting Needs with Home Equity Financing

We’ve earned the trust of our communities and have long been committed to delivering what’s important to them. We are meeting our customers’ needs and helping fulfill dreams – whatever they may be. Home improvements, debt consolidation, education or a long-overdue vacation can be successfully financed with the right home equity loan.

Home Equity Line of Credit

Bogota Credit Union offers instant purchasing power and flexibility with a home equity line of credit (HELOC) that fits your specific needs. With access to cash at any time, a line of credit is a valuable financing option for large purchases or expenses that are needed immediately. Education tuition, home renovation projects or unforeseen medical expenses are typical expenses that may benefit from home equity lending. Visit your local branch or call (201) 862-8260 Ext 1124. You can also choose to take advantage of our Online Application or download a hard copy of our HELOC Application.
